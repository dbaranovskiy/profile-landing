About Handy Dandy

Our Story

Welcome to Handy Dandy! We started this company with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality handyman services to our community. Frustrated by the lack of dependable professionals for small to medium-sized home projects, our founder, John Dandy, decided to build a business based on trust, craftsmanship, and excellent customer service.

Experience & Credentials

With over 20 years of experience in various trades, Handy Dandy is a licensed and insured handyman service. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our commitment to getting the job done right the first time.

Commitment to Customers in the DMV

Your home is your most important asset, and we treat it with the care and respect it deserves. We are dedicated to providing honest pricing and earning 5-star reviews from our valued customers across DC, Maryland, and Virginia.
